Run several sessions at once in a preset grid. Drag any sidebar row onto a pane to place it there, and pick a shape from the title bar: single, `1|1`, `2|1`, `1|2`, or `2|2` (each number is how many panes are stacked in that column). Sidebar rows badge the pane they occupy, each pane keeps its own tab, and the layout survives a restart.
